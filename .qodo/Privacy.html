Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Blood Donation Website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information while you use our platform.

We Collect

When you register as a donor, request blood, or subscribe to newsletters, we may collect personally identifiable information such as your name, contact details, blood group, location, and health information relevant to donation eligibility.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used solely for the purpose of facilitating blood donation and requests, communicating important updates, and improving our services. We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties.

Data Sharing

To expedite blood donation and delivery, some of your information (like name, blood group, and contact details) may be shared with hospitals, patients, and authorized partners strictly on a need-to-know basis under secure protocols.

Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience by tracking user visits and preferences. Cookies do not collect personal information outside of usage data and can be managed through your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information stored on our platform. You may contact us anytime to update or remove your details.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child’s information has been submitted, please contact us immediately for removal.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
